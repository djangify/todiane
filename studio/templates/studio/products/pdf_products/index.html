    Do I need Canva Pro to edit the templates?

    No, the free version of Canva works perfectly. Some templates may use Pro elements which you can easily swap for free alternatives. The core templates are fully editable with a free Canva account.

    Can I sell products I create with these templates?

    Yes! The commercial license allows you to use these templates in products you sell. You can customise them with your branding and sell as your own. You just can't resell the original template files themselves.

    What file formats are included?

    Each product includes ready-to-use PDF files plus a link to the editable Canva template. From Canva, you can export to PDF, PNG, JPG, or print directly. Some products also include PNG versions.

    Are the digital planners compatible with GoodNotes/Notability?

    Yes! The digital planners include hyperlinked tabs that work in GoodNotes, Notability, PDF Expert, and similar apps. They're designed for iPad but also work on other tablets and desktops.

    Do you take custom requests?

    Yes, I do take on custom PDF and template projects. Get in touch via the contact form with details about what you need, and I'll let you know if it's something I can help with.
