    {% endblock %} {% block faq_content %} {# FAQ 1 #}

    What does "offline-first" mean?

    The software runs entirely on your computer without needing an internet connection. Your financial data never leaves your device — it's stored locally in a database file that you control. No accounts, no cloud sync, no data harvesting.
    {# FAQ 2 #}

    Is this suitable for UK self-assessment?

    Yes! The software includes categories aligned with HMRC self-assessment boxes for self-employed income and expenses. Reports are designed to give you the figures you need for your tax return. However, this is a bookkeeping tool, not tax software — you'll still submit your return through HMRC or your accountant.
    {# FAQ 3 #}

    Can I use this on multiple computers?

    Your license covers personal use on your own computers. You can install it on your desktop and laptop. To sync data between machines, simply copy the database file using your preferred method (USB drive, Dropbox, etc.).
    {# FAQ 4 #}

    What if I lose my data?

    The software includes a backup feature that creates a copy of your database file. We recommend backing up regularly and storing copies somewhere safe — an external drive, cloud storage, or both. You're in control of your backups, just like you're in control of your data.
