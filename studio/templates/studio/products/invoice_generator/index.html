    Can I customise the invoice templates?

    Absolutely. The templates are built with Django and Tailwind CSS, so you can customise colours, fonts, layouts, and add your logo. The documentation includes guidance on making common customisations.

    Does this include payment processing?

    This tool focuses on creating and tracking invoices. Payment processing is not included — you can add your payment details to invoices and track payments manually, or extend the system to integrate with payment providers if needed.

    Can I use this for VAT invoices?

    Yes, the system supports VAT and tax calculations. You can set your VAT rate and the templates include proper VAT number display and breakdown. Suitable for UK and EU businesses.

    What hosting do I need?

    Any VPS that runs Python/Django works well. Railway, Digital Ocean, or Hetzner are all good options. A basic £5-10/month server is plenty for personal or small business use.

    Is there a limit on invoices?

    No limits whatsoever. Create as many invoices, clients, and PDF exports as you need. It's your system on your server — you're only limited by your hosting resources.
