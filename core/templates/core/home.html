About Diane Corriette

Supporting Independent Creators

I’m Diane Corriette, a web developer who builds practical eCommerce and personal-growth systems using Python, Django, and TailwindCSS. My focus is helping independent creators and small businesses simplify the tech that powers their ideas, so they can focus on creating and earning, not troubleshooting.

For more than a decade I built WordPress websites, helping businesses share their work and sell their expertise online. In 2023 I decided to go deeper, learning to code so I could create the tools behind the sites I used to build. That decision opened a new chapter for me as a full-stack developer.

I enjoy using Django not just for eCommerce, but to design small, purposeful tools that support growth and learning. Each project is an opportunity to build something useful and well-structured for others who create online. Connect with me on LinkedIn if you’d like to discuss ideas or collaborations.
