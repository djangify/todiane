Software Studio

About This Studio

I'm Diane Corriette, an indie software and product developer. I build practical tools: offline-first apps, Django-powered platforms, creative coding projects, and digital products that help people work, learn, and create with clarity.

This studio is where everything comes together: the experiments, the finished products, the long-term projects, and the ideas still being shaped.

Indie & Bootstrapped

I develop everything independently. The code, the design, the deployment, the packaging, and the products.

What I Build

Offline-First Apps

Tools that run entirely on the user's device — no accounts, no subscriptions, complete ownership of data.

Micro-SaaS Utilities

Small, purposeful online tools that solve focused problems with clean UX and long-term maintainability.

Digital Workbooks

Journaling tools, personal development guides, writing prompts, and practical frameworks for everyday growth.

Ecommerce & Learning Platforms

Django frameworks used to build real stores, membership sites, and video-based learning systems.

Creative Coding

Interactive tools, animations, and playful experiments that bring joy and curiosity back to software.

Research & Prototypes

New ideas, frameworks, and early concepts that may become full products or shape future tools.

How I Work

Simplicity First

I avoid unnecessary complexity and build systems people actually enjoy using.

Privacy & Ownership

Offline-first tools give people control over their data — no servers required.

Indie & Independent

No investors, no deadlines — just focused, intentional product development.

Long-Term Thinking

I build products meant to last — not trends, not hype, but durable tools.

Clarity & Structure

Interfaces that reduce mental load and make work feel smoother.

Experimentation

The Studio is also a playground — ideas start here before becoming products.

Why This Studio Exists

After years of creating quietly and independently, I chose to build a space where all the tools, experiments, and meaningful projects could live together. This Studio is that place — a home for practical software, thoughtful design, and creative curiosity.

I build to support purpose, clarity, and independence — in myself and in the people who use my tools.
