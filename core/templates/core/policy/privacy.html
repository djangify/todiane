1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how AI Marketing Platform ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information. This policy applies to all services provided through our website, including our blog, newsletter, and shop.

By using our website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and password.
  • Contact Information: When using our contact form, we collect your name and email address.
  • Newsletter Subscriptions: Email addresses for newsletter subscribers.
  • Shopping Information: For purchases, we collect:
    • Name and email address
    • Billing and shipping addresses
    • Phone number
    • Payment information (processed securely by Stripe and PayPal)
  • Technical Information: We automatically collect:
    • IP address
    • Browser type and version
    • Device information
    • Pages visited and interaction data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our services
  • To process and fulfill orders
  • To send newsletters and marketing communications
  • To respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • To improve our website and services
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Information Sharing

We share your information with:

  • Payment Processors: Stripe and PayPal for processing payments
  • Email Service Providers: For newsletter delivery and communication
  • Analytics Providers: To understand website usage

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Object to data processing

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure payment processing through trusted providers
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Limited access to personal information by authorized personnel

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect or maintain information from children under 13.
