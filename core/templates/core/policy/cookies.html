1. Overview

This Cookie Policy explains how AI Marketing Platform uses cookies and similar technologies on our website. We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience, provide secure shopping features, and improve our services.

By continuing to use our website, you agree to our use of cookies as described in this policy.

2. Essential Cookies

Essential cookies are necessary for our website to function properly. These include:

  • Session cookies for managing user sessions
  • CSRF security tokens for form submission protection
  • Authentication cookies to maintain your login status
  • Shopping cart session cookies

These cookies are strictly necessary and cannot be disabled as they ensure core website functionality and security.

3. Functional Cookies

Functional cookies help enhance your experience by remembering your preferences:

  • User interface preferences
  • Newsletter subscription status
  • Previously viewed shop items
  • Login remember-me functionality

4. Shop Cookies

Our shop functionality uses cookies to:

  • Maintain your shopping cart contents
  • Remember your shipping preferences
  • Track order progress
  • Facilitate secure checkout processes
  • Store product preferences and history

5. Third-Party Cookies

We use third-party services that may set cookies:

  • Payment Processing:
    • Stripe cookies for secure payment processing
    • PayPal cookies for payment functionality
  • Analytics: For website usage analysis and improvement
  • Newsletter Service: For managing email subscriptions

These third-party services have their own privacy and cookie policies which control how they use this information.

6. Cookie Duration

Our cookies have varying lifespans:

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies:
    • Authentication cookies: 14 days (with "Remember Me")
    • Shopping cart: 7 days
    • User preferences: 30 days

7. Cookie Management

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Here's how to manage cookies in popular browsers:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies

Please note that disabling essential cookies will affect the website's core functionality and shopping features.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for operational and legal requirements. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

For questions about our Cookie Policy, please contact us at support@aimarketingplatform.app
