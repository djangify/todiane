1. Introduction

This AI Writing Policy explains how artificial intelligence tools are used to support my content creation and coding process and how I ensure that all final content is accurate and trustworthy.

2. How I Use AI

I use AI tools as part of my coding and writing workflow to help with:

  • Structuring articles, guides, and resources more efficiently
  • Rewriting and updating older content (originally created between 2010â€“2016)
  • Creating first drafts of new content or code
  • Debugging existing code

AI is treated as a supportive tool, not a replacement for human expertise.

3. Human Oversight & Editing

All AI-assisted drafts are reviewed, fact-checked, and edited by a human before being published. I make sure that the tone, accuracy, and practicality of every piece meet the standards of my brand.

The final responsibility for the accuracy and integrity of any content rests with me or another human in the majority of cases.

4. Why This Matters

I believe it is important to be clear about how my content is created. While AI helps to save time and stay productive, every resource shared is human-checked to ensure reliability. This balance helps me create practical, actionable content without compromising quality or ethics.

5. Updating & Transparency

If this approach to using AI changes in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly. You can always refer back to this page to see the most recent version of this AI Writing Policy.
